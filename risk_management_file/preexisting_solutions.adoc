= List of Currently-Existing Tools/Solutions

A variety of scores/tools currently exist which assess bleeding/ischaemia risk based on patient characteristics. Some of these include:

* ARC HBR
* PreciseDAPT
* DAPT Score
* HasBLED
* REACH
* Dutch ASA
* BleeMACS
